About Us

Our values

Historically our hotel used to be a large Victorian manor. In 2010 it was donated by Donkey and Shrek and was renovated into a luxurious hotel. Staff are welcoming and friendly. Gorden Ramsey is our world class chef who produces the most exquisite and unique dishes.We have an extensive list of options to choose from for your canapes, wedding breakfast and evening buffet. Always striving to ensure everyone is catered for, they work extremely hard to prepare dishes which cater for any dietary requirement.

From ensuring the gardens look beautiful all year round, giving the bedrooms a fresh lick of paint, stocking up the bar (most important), or writing these fantastic little numbers, The manor consists of 20 rooms each with an en-suite, tea/coffee facility and views of the garden below. We do specialise in events and are a popular choice as a wedding venue and can provide full catering. The hotel maintains the old wooden beams and traditional fabric of an ancient and historic building while adding contemporary design features and modern comforts, with understated British style.

We welcome you to stay with us and make sure all your needs are met!
